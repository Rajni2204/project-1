Our Vision

To reach out to vulnerable street children through
our network of various charitable organizations that enable
them to become self reliant and responsible citizens.

Our Mission

To empower all underprivileged children of Mumbai by
providing them access to education, recreation and a healthy
environment with the help of which they can build a steady
and friendlyfuture for themselves and to set up a network that
will take care of every child in need.
